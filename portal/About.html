ABOUT CBSE PORTAL

CBSE PORTAL is a tool designed to assist teachers to reduce their work and to increase efficiency
without any extra efforts. The day to day notes and announcements are uploaded every day.
This will be of immense help for those students who missed class due to unavoidable reasons
and reduce their hardship to collects the notes by various means.


CBSE PORTAL is an effort towards quality work of teacher and students
as records are converted to digital format.
